Dear Reader,

I wholeheartedly believe that our culture and beliefs shape the way we see the world and move through it. Many of us have been taught to put everything before ourselves. To work our way out of our pain. To use success as a marker for happiness. To see health within our muscles and bones and nowhere else. But, we must change this not only for ourselves but for the generations after us.

Strength does not lie within suffering in silence.
Peace does not come from assimilation.

Who are you?
What are your needs?
How do you need to be loved?

We all deserve to learn how to treat our mind
and bodies with respect and dignity.
