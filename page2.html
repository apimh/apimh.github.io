Dear Reader,

My journey with mental health has been pretty complicated. The older I get, the more complex things become. The more I learn that I have to deal not only with my own mental health, but also the way that is tied to generational trauma. Which means confronting not only my elders, but my entire community and the conversations we do or don't have about healing.

There are many discussions that I am still building up the courage to have, and I hope to begin them here. I hope to build some softness here that I can share with you all while also acknowledging my own privilege.

Sometimes therapists are not the right fit, sometimes I have to question the things that tradition has taught me and choose what is right for myself, sometimes there are months where I do not have the energy to show up for myself.

But this, here, is me trying to show up and be transparent for myself. And you.
