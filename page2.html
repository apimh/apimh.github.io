about

Hi, I'm Victoria and my journey with mental health has been pretty complicated. The older I get, the more complex things become. The more I learn that I have to deal not only with my own mental health, but also the way that is tied to generational trauma. Which means confronting not only my elders, but my entire community and the conversations we do or don't have about healing. 

There are many discussions that I am still building up the courage to have. And I hope to have them here. I hope to build some softness here that I can share with you all. 

I also want to acknowledge the barriers to get help with your mental health. People of color are already asked to do so much. So much bravery is required to even admit that we are struggling but past that... who can afford therapy? Who has access to therapists that speak their mother tongue? Who has the ability to take time off of work and caretaking in order to see a therapist? I want to acknowledge these barriers and keep them in mind as I continue to navigate through my own situation.
